How to Play

  1. A target time is shown — anywhere from 1 to 25 seconds.
  2. A 3-2-1 countdown begins, then the screen turns red.
  3. Tap the screen when you think that exact amount of time has passed.
  4. Your score is how far off you were. Lower is better.

Daily — 5 rounds. Everyone gets the same times. One attempt per day.
Practice — Play as many rounds as you like.
